About the FDP

The Faculty Development Programme (FDP) is organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Information Technology (IT) at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), Noida. The program aims to enhance the academic and research skills of faculty members in the emerging areas of Computer Science, including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity.

Schedule

  • Day 1: Inauguration & Keynote on AI
  • Day 2: Workshops on Machine Learning
  • Day 3: Cloud Computing Applications
  • Day 4: Cybersecurity Trends
  • Day 5: Research Methodology & Closing

Speakers

  • Dr. A. Sharma (IIT Delhi) - Artificial Intelligence
  • Prof. B. Verma (IIT Kanpur) - Machine Learning
  • Ms. C. Gupta (Microsoft) - Cloud Computing
  • Mr. D. Singh (Cybersecurity Expert) - Cybersecurity
