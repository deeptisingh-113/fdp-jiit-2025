Faculty Development Programme (FDP)

Next-Gen AI: Cutting-Edge Trends in ML and Generative AI

30th June – 5th July 2025 | JIIT Noida

Programme Objective

The FDP titled “Autonomous AI for Sustainable Development” aims to equip educators and researchers with practical knowledge, tools, and strategies to implement and teach advanced AI techniques contributing to Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Key Topics

  • AI-powered Automation and AutoML
  • Quantum Machine Learning (QML)
  • Deep Generative Models (GANs, VAEs, Diffusion Models)
  • Agentic AI and Large Language Models (GPT, BERT, LLaMA)
  • AI in Healthcare, Cybersecurity, and Education
  • Trustworthy and Explainable AI
  • Hands-on ML & GenAI using Python
